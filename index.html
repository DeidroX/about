ABOUTME

Who am I?

Good day everyone, my name is Nino “DeidroX” Righi. I’m 26 years old. I live in Augsburg (near Munich), Germany and I'm a Jr. Frontend Developer and I design and develop my own Video Games. I’m currently attending the University of Augsburg as a computer science (B.Sc.) student.

What am I doing exactly?

For the moment I'm diving deep into the world of Web Development by consuming excellent video-tutorials provided by Frontend Masters. In addition to that I work on my own little Web Projects and I design and develop indie video games using the Unreal Engine 4. You can find my projects here.

What is my motivation?

For the Web Development part it is enough to say that I would do it as a job on a regular basis.

Game Development as a whole combines aspects of musics, soundeffects, arts, mathematics, communication, interaction, literature/history, storytelling and many things more, to create something with an incredible depth, which you can rarely experience elsewhere. Combined with my passion to do something creative and seeing my ideas become real, playable scenarios, is just everything I've ever wanted.
